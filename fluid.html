Testing Details

Located on the Basement -1 of Block-A (Room no. -1A02).

Covers a floor area of 767.5 sft.

It is well equipped with many sophisticated equipment like Laboratory Flume with the provision of using Broad Crested Weir, Parshall Flume, Venturi Flume, Sluice Gate, and full set of Hydraulic Bench with provision of using Venturi Meter, Orifice, Mouthpiece and Toroidal Quadrant.

This lab is designed for carrying out a clear visual appreciation of basic hydraulic principles like Centre of Pressure, discharge measurement, Major and Minor loss of pipe flow.
